Professional

I am a Developer Advocate who currently works for IBM in Austin, Texas, originally born in Houston. I have been primarily focused on iOS mobile software, but I am grateful to learn about web technology more recently, such as Node.js.

My favorite thing to do is to teach. I firmly believe that the best teaching moments are the ones where you learn the most where you previously knew very little. I try very hard to take what I learn, and post it on GitHub, but I think there is more value in being able to explain how something works and walk someone through it.
